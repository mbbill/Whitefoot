# Round 2 — Type inference & implicit conversions (`type-inference`)

**Verdict:** `needs_evidence`

## Rationale

The feature as bundled is largely dispatched, but its live core is undecidable on this corpus, so the honest verdict is needs_evidence with a decided floor. What is decided: (1) Implicit conversions are cut — the prosecutor's COUNT 3 is fully card-backed (N001/N002: conversions silently reassign per-operation optimizer contracts; D010/D001: they perturb dispatch and specialization) and the defender conceded everything but a zero-runtime-effect subsumption sliver, which the null hypothesis also excludes absent evidence. (2) Whole-program/cross-boundary inference is cut — D009's silent degradation, J001/J002's reconstruction machinery, non-local blame, and the defender's own concession; every signature, interface, instantiation, mode, and effect is mandatorily explicit, consistent with all four round1 leading candidates. (3) The named alternative is REJECTED in its literal form: 'layout and mode at every binding' is contradicted by the corpus's strongest architecture cards (F007/J004: representation is analysis-conditional; F002: pinning forfeits the exact documented payoffs; F009/A002/A006: keep representation decisions late and separable), and 'every binding and call' founders on D001-unnameable types without new machinery neither side priced. The prosecutor's own filed 'boundary-drawing risk' concedes the decisive blur: its permitted 'bottom-up checking from annotated leaves' and the defender's 'local inference inside annotated boundaries' differ only in whether a let-binding restates a type the checker already uniquely determines. That residual question — is same-pass interior annotation independent checked redundancy that catches AI error (prosecutor COUNT 2), or correlated noise costing O(k^2) tokens and edit cascades (defender horn b) — is precisely an AI-writer empirical question, flagged EMPIRICAL-UNTESTED by both parties, sitting in a gap the corpus itself documents (round1-aliasing missing-cards note; the numeric round's 'uncarded fourth channel' of compiler-inferred facts, which also caps how much the prosecutor's 'stated facts are load-bearing' premise can claim). Ruling it either way today would violate the standing rule that proposals cite evidence and state what remains unproven. Leading candidate carried forward pending experiments: boundary-mandatory, intent-explicit core — zero implicit conversions, all intent facts explicit at boundaries, no per-binding layout, interior types produced by deterministic ambiguity-rejecting local synthesis with mandatory compiler-emitted elaboration records serving re-reads and near-duplicate consistency diffing. The experiments below decide whether interior annotation becomes mandatory (flipping to the prosecutor's regime minus layout) or elision stands.

## Optimizer role

Split by sub-feature. (1) Implicit value-changing conversions are optimizer-NEGATIVE and are ruled out of core regardless of any future experiment: N001/N002 establish that numeric semantics are per-operation optimizer contracts (wrapping vs nuw/nsw-poison), so a compiler-inserted conversion silently swaps which contract governs with no source token, and D010 shows conversions perturb which method is selected and specialized (D001). This is incompatible with the numeric-semantics round's leading candidate (named, truthfully-lowered modes) — an implicit conversion is unnamed by construction. Both parties effectively agree here; the defender conceded all value-changing conversions. (2) Type inference per se contributes nothing the optimizer consumes: optimizer facts are boundary/intent annotations (ownership modes per F001/C004, numeric modes per N002, effects, shapes/layouts per A001) plus compiler analysis (N004-style derived facts can exceed writer precision). Inference only determines which CHANNEL — writer text or checker derivation — produces interior type facts. The corpus's own dispatch-generics candidate keeps one implicit path (guaranteed static specialization), establishing the governing principle: compiler-implicit is acceptable when the choice is guaranteed and semantics-invariant, unacceptable when it selects semantics. Local synthesis of a uniquely-determined interior type is the former; implicit conversion is the latter. (3) The alternative's per-binding LAYOUT/MODE mandate is itself optimizer-negative and rejected: F007/J004 show allocation/representation decisions are analysis-conditional; F002's payoffs (register promotion, load/store elimination) require the optimizer to own interior representation; F009/A002/A006 evidence the winning architecture of keeping representation and schedule decisions late and separable. No card evidences a per-binding layout mandate, and the null hypothesis applies to mandates too.

## Verification role

This is where the verdict genuinely hinges, and the corpus cannot resolve it. Prosecutor COUNT 2 (mandatory interior annotations are checked redundancy — two derivations that must agree, converting hallucination into a localized type error) is the strongest keep-the-alternative argument; the defender's rebuttal (same-pass annotations are error-correlated with the code they annotate, so the redundancy has near-zero independence and its diagnostic 'annotation is ground truth' heuristic is unsound) is mechanistically plausible via autoregressive conditioning. Both are EMPIRICAL-UNTESTED; the corpus itself flags the gap (round1-aliasing-model.md: no cards on annotation error rates or inference feasibility for internal functions). What CAN be ruled: (a) F001/F003's warning — unchecked writer promises are a miscompilation channel — means any annotation the optimizer assumes must be checked, so annotations are either checked-redundant or unsound-assumed; there is no free third state. (b) D009's silent-degradation exhibit convicts only inference with a dynamic fallback: in a static core where local synthesis is deterministic and ambiguity is a hard error, inference failure is a compile error, not silent dynamic dispatch — the defender's boundary-annotation regime disarms D009's negative scope (heuristic failure under abstract types). (c) Whole-program inference across unannotated boundaries is cut on verification grounds: non-local blame, J001/J002 show reconstruction channels carry guard/deopt machinery, and the defender concedes it. (d) Explicit conversions make every representation change a stated, checkable fact — verification-positive. (e) Trust architecture: C004's auto traits show compiler-DERIVED facts (checker-trusted) versus its negative scope showing manual assertions are the UB channel; under a least-trusted-writer charter, moving derived-fact production into the checker is a verification improvement, provided derived facts are exported in a trusted, machine-readable elaborated form.

## AI-writer considerations

All admissible writer-cost claims on both sides are EMPIRICAL-UNTESTED — the corpus contains zero AI-codegen evidence cards; every statement below is flagged as such. Prosecutor's admissible costs of full annotation: generation/context token inflation (the defender's O(k^2) instantiated-type arithmetic for D001-style pipelines is structurally sound; its practical weight depends on unmeasured code-shape frequency), edit cascades scaling with program size not change size, more compile-error iterations, and unnameable types (closures, monomorphized instantiations) requiring new naming machinery — though Ada-style explicit nominal instantiation shows this is a cost, not an impossibility, and aligns with the dispatch-generics explicit-instantiation candidate. Defender's admissible costs of elision: re-reading AIs must re-derive interior types (a hallucination source) unless the toolchain emits compiler-elaborated fully-annotated views — this mitigation is a hard tooling requirement, and any workflow reading raw source without the elaborator loses it. Drift cuts both ways and is untested in both directions: inference-side, near-duplicate fragments can silently diverge in inferred types from a one-token difference (annotation converts this to an error at the drift site); annotation-side, wrong-but-well-typed annotations at elaboration-driving sites (let x: u8 = 255) are silent semantic decisions, and full annotation maximizes the count of such sites — though explicit literal typing is required under either regime, making this partially a wash. Machine-readable structured diagnostics are a hard requirement under EITHER regime: full annotation multiplies diagnostic volume; local inference must report ambiguity errors with elaboration provenance. Costs credited to cutting inference: no constraint-solver compile time in the iteration loop, no need for the writer to simulate coercion-resolution order to predict what its own code means.

## What must be true

- DECIDED FLOOR (holds regardless of experiment outcome): zero implicit value-changing conversions in core — every representation or numeric-mode change is an explicit, named, mode-carrying operation (N001/N002/D010); even checker-proven zero-runtime-effect subsumption stays out under the null hypothesis unless re-proposed with evidence, and if admitted must emit a machine-readable elaboration record, never be silent.
- DECIDED FLOOR: all intent-bearing facts are mandatory and explicit at every function signature, module interface, and generic instantiation — numeric mode, ownership/aliasing capability, effect row, and ABI-visible/public layout at type and interface declarations (F001/F003/C004/N002/F009; round1 aliasing-model, dispatch-generics, numeric-semantics, concurrency leading candidates). No cross-boundary inference of any of these, ever.
- DECIDED FLOOR: the alternative's per-binding layout/mode mandate is rejected — interior representation is optimizer-owned (F002/F007/J004/F009/A002/A006); a schedule/layout layer, if added, is separable (A006), not fused into every binding.
- If interior type elision is kept (defender's sliver): local synthesis must be deterministic, non-backtracking, defaulting-free (literal types explicit), with ambiguity a hard compile error — this is what disarms D009; any dynamic or heuristic fallback re-opens silent performance loss and voids this ruling.
- If interior type elision is kept: the compiler MUST emit a canonical, machine-readable fully-elaborated form (every type/mode/effect explicit) as the re-read artifact for AI writers AND as the substrate for a consistency checker that diffs elaborations of near-duplicate fragments — this is the required consistency-checking mechanism for repeated code; without it the prosecutor's inference-side drift scenario stands unmitigated.
- If interior annotations are instead mandated (prosecutor's regime minus layout): the language must provide nominal naming machinery for currently-unnameable types (explicit instantiation declarations, named closure types), and the redundancy-independence experiment must have shown same-pass annotations actually catch writer errors at rates justifying the measured token/cascade costs.
- The IR verifier (ir-strategy candidate) must reject facts not originating from stated-and-checked or checker-derived sources, closing the back door of inference/coercion leaking in via macros, codegen templates, or convenience front-ends (prosecutor's toolchain-consistency point, unrebutted).
- Round1 mode vocabularies (ownership, capabilities, numeric modes, effects) must stabilize before mass code generation in either regime; annotation-vocabulary churn is a systemic rewrite risk that scales with annotation density.
- Card caveat: D/A/C/J citations (D001/D009/D010/C004/A001-A006/J001-J004) are direct-extraction and not adversarially verified per caveats.md and round1-index; the sub-rulings leaning on them (especially the D009 disarming and the layout rejection's J004 leg) must be re-checked when those cards are verified.

## Follow-ups

- Redundancy-independence experiment (decides the verdict): matched AI codegen tasks under two regimes — mandatory interior type annotations vs elided interiors with boundary annotations; measure (a) rate at which interior annotations catch real semantic errors the elided regime would compile wrong, (b) rate of correlated wrongness (annotation+code both wrong, typechecks), (c) trivial-mismatch friction errors and iterations-to-green. Directly tests prosecutor COUNT 2 vs defender horn (b).
- Token/context economics: total tokens generated plus context consumed for read-modify-write tasks under full annotation vs boundary annotation plus compiler-elaborated views, including deep generic pipeline shapes (tests the O(k^2) claim's practical weight) and re-read error rates with raw source vs elaborated views (tests the defender's mitigation dependency).
- Consistency-drift experiment: near-duplicate AI-generated fragments differing by one token (e.g., a literal); measure silent type/representation divergence rates under inferred vs annotated regimes and what fraction each regime's checker catches vs coherent wrongness that typechecks (tests the prosecutor's core drift claim in both directions).
- Edit-cascade measurement: propagate one boundary type/effect/mode change; measure edit volume, induced hallucination rate per regenerated site, and loop iterations under both regimes (tests regeneration-cost-scales-with-program-size).
- Compile-time delta in the AI iteration loop: syntax-directed checking of fully annotated programs vs deterministic local synthesis at scale, for a type system carrying ownership, effects, and modes (direction is argued structural, magnitude unmeasured; D004 covers only codegen-unit/LTO).
- Execute the already-filed numeric backlog item (missing-research-backlog.jsonl, numeric follow-up): measure how much nsw/range-grade optimization LLVM recovers with no frontend flags from types and dominating guards — the 'uncarded fourth channel' that reprices the 'stated facts are load-bearing' premise for both sides.

- Adversarially verify the load-bearing direct-extraction cards this ruling leans on (D001, D009, D010, C004, J004, A001, A002, A006) per the round1-index standing note, before any regime decision is finalized.
