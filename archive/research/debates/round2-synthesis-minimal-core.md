## Minimal core sketch

- SURFACE: exactly one canonical form — one spelling per semantic construct, one legal byte-level formatting, grammar isomorphic to the checked core tree; non-canonical input is a hard error, never auto-formatted; the stored artifact is fully elaborated (every type/mode/effect/contract explicit) and is the only stored spelling [syntax, type-inference].
- COMPILATION MODEL: closed-world, whole-program, no stable internal ABI, no interposition, no separate semantic compilation; incrementality is implementation-level via content-addressed exact semantic fingerprints; exactly one explicit FFI wall with declared fact-attenuation (currently UNDESIGNED — a named soundness precondition, not a detail) [modules; D004/D007/D008].
- TYPES AND FACTS: concrete static types everywhere at boundaries — every function signature, module-interface-equivalent, and instantiation carries explicit ownership/aliasing capability, effect row, numeric mode, and ABI-visible layout; zero cross-boundary inference; whether INTERIOR let-bindings are annotated or produced by deterministic, defaulting-free, ambiguity-rejecting local synthesis is the one open regime question (needs_evidence) [type-inference; F001/F003/N002/C004].
- GENERICS: monomorphization-only parametric generics, compiler-side expansion always, explicit instantiation arguments (no inference/bound-solving in the trust path), templates type-checked once against declared bounds before instantiation, no polymorphic recursion or higher-rank parameters, no IR-level generic ABI or witness tables; per-type divergence only via explicit individually-checked specializations with coherence rules [generics; D001/D005/F009 axis held open].
- NUMERICS: a small vocabulary of named, truthfully-lowered numeric modes, each arithmetic node naming exactly one mode; zero implicit value-changing conversions — every representation change is an explicit, named, mode-carrying operation [type-inference floor; N001/N002/D010].
- DISPATCH: sum types with compiler-checked exhaustive match (wildcard arms banned in checked contexts) as the only dispatch mechanism in core; typed operation tables (named fields, per-row signature checks, construction-checked payload/table coupling, one canonical table per variant-set×operation) as the blessed data-driven form; NO dyn/vtables/trait objects in core — open-set dispatch is remanded to the FFI periphery [dynamic-dispatch; D001/D002/D009].
- FUNCTION VALUES: explicit env structs passed as type parameters implementing a call interface, monomorphized to guaranteed direct calls; env-field types (owned/&/&mut) ARE the declared capture modes; no capture inference, no anonymous escaping environments; escaping/heterogeneous sets only via closed tag-enums (record-of-closures is the residual idiom, cost unmeasured) [closures; D009].
- MEMORY: every allocation carries a writer-stated, compiler-checked storage/lifetime contract (ownership, with a to-be-carded region/arena layer); deallocation is compiler-derived but surfaced as explicit IR drop ops plus machine-readable per-function lifetime reports — no GC, no ambient RC, no invisible drop; 'cleanup always runs' is explicitly NOT an invariant [memory-automation, error-handling; F001/F006/C004/F009].
- ERRORS: Result<T,E> monomorphized to concrete branch-based code with verifier-checked propagation and auto-derived context/cause chains, plus trap-with-report=abort for contract violations, with descriptive stack-attribution metadata (CFI/frame pointers) but no unwinding execution semantics, no catch, no drop-during-unwind [error-handling; F008/C002/A005].
- COMPILE-TIME COMPUTATION: a closed, decidable, trivially-terminating constant-expression sublanguage (built-in arithmetic/layout/target-parameter constants only); no user-defined compile-time evaluation, no macros, no staging; facts about constant data are compiler-derived by inspection, never writer-asserted [metaprogramming; F006/N004].
- CONTRACT KERNEL (required under every open outcome): named contract groups (signature + law bundles) with per-member conformance checking, and type-level capability predicates (Send/Sync-class, C004) — the interface-shaped machinery both interfaces-trial parties converge on; whether full bounded quantification beyond the kept generics core exists is needs_evidence [interfaces].
- VERIFICATION SPINE: the verifier-checked IR (round-1 candidate) rejects any optimizer fact not originating from a stated-and-checked or checker-derived source; law/effect annotations must be checked obligations, not trusted assertions — there is no third state between checked-redundant and unsound-assumed [ir-strategy coupling; F003/N006].
- PENDING HOLES the core sketch cannot yet close: interior-annotation regime (type-inference experiment), bounded-quantification form (interfaces experiment + mono/shared measurement), FFI fact-attenuation design, region/arena exemplar evidence, and the charter question of predictable-cost-model vs guaranteed-optimization-floor.

## Cut list

- Implicit value-changing conversions/coercions of any kind, including compiler-inserted numeric widening (decided floor, both parties agreed) [type-inference; N001/N002/D010].
- Whole-program / cross-boundary type inference and any inference of intent-bearing facts (ownership, effects, modes, layout) across signatures [type-inference; D009 silent-degradation].
- Dynamic dispatch in core: dyn constructs, compiler-synthesized vtables, fat pointers, unsizing coercions, dyn-compatibility rule subsystem (D003), open implementer sets [dynamic-dispatch; D002/D007/D008].
- Closures as a language feature: capture inference, capture lists, anonymous closure types, closure conversion in the trusted pipeline, escaping environments; Function-typed fields with hidden call targets made inexpressible [closures; D009, F004 negative scope].
- All metaprogramming: syntactic/token/AST macros, user-visible CTFE/comptime, code-producing stages, derive/annotation processors as core features [metaprogramming; F006 restriction pattern, ir-strategy soundness rule].
- Human-oriented syntax: infix/precedence, sugar, alternate spellings, elision of contract-bearing facts, resugaring diagnostics, pretty-printer write paths (a bijective infix codec is admissible only later, behind the gating A/B experiment) [syntax].
- Exceptions and unwinding: throw/catch, zero-cost EH tables as execution semantics, landingpad/funclet lowering, drop-during-unwind, the 'cleanup always runs' invariant; foreign unwinds abort at the FFI boundary (provisional, evidence-free) [error-handling; F008, refuted rows 1-2].
- Module system as semantics: compilation units with stable internal ABI, symbol interposition, module interface files, separately-compiled-and-optimized units (codegen units survive only as build artifacts) [modules; D004/D007/D008].
- Automatic memory management: tracing GC, pervasive/default RC-ARC, invisible implicit drop, finalizers [memory-automation; F007/J004 conditional-recovery, F009].
- From the kept generics core: instantiation inference, bound solving, polymorphic recursion, higher-rank parameters, IR-level generic ABI/witness tables/shared bodies (deferred behind F003-discharge check and measurement, not cut forever) [generics].
- Wildcard/default arms in checked dispatch contexts; index-addressed/positional operation tables; void*/untyped erasure in any sanctioned core form [dynamic-dispatch].
- Unrestricted runtime reflection/metadata (per-construct capability declaration required instead) [modules; D005 negative scope].
- Also REJECTED though it was an 'alternative': the per-binding layout/mode mandate — interior representation stays optimizer-owned [type-inference; F002/F007/J004/F009].

## Replacements the language must provide

- UNIFIED CONSISTENCY-CHECKING LAYER (specified once; six verdicts depend on it — syntax, metaprogramming, dynamic-dispatch, closures, type-inference, interfaces, memory-automation). Substrate: the byte-canonical fully-elaborated artifact makes identity syntactic, so the compiler content-addresses every subtree. Four escalating mechanisms over that substrate: (1) IDENTITY BY CONSTRUCTION — anything parametric over types is a generic template, expanded only in-compiler; copies never exist in source, so no check is needed (generics verdict: generation-by-construction dominates check-after-generation). (2) PROVENANCE-CHECKED GENERATION — any other intended-identical or pattern-instantiated region carries machine-readable provenance (generator identity, canonical inputs, content hash); the build re-runs the deterministic EXTERNAL generator and diffs against the source of record; divergence fails the build (metaprogramming). (3) ONE-DEFINITION RULES — one canonical operation table per (variant-set, operation) with ad-hoc switch sites lowered to it or flagged (dynamic-dispatch); dedup detection of structurally identical env-struct/function pairs across modules (closures). (4) ELABORATION-DIFFING — the compiler's canonical elaborated records of near-duplicate fragments are diffed to catch silent type/mode divergence from one-token edits (type-inference). Diagnostics from all four aggregate N copy-local errors into ONE pattern-level repair site via provenance.
- Replacing dyn dispatch: sum types + exhaustive match with banned wildcards, plus typed operation tables with named fields, per-row signature checking, construction-checked payload/table coupling (payload X with table Y is a type error), payloads inside the ownership/F003 discipline; on variant-set change the compiler emits a machine-readable site-enumerated edit list (file, site, missing arm/row) as a mechanical repair plan.
- Replacing closures: the env-struct + monomorphized call-interface encoding as a GUARANTEED language capability (never fn-pointer+env indirection), plus mandated core ERRORS reconstructing capture-list exactness — unused env field, used-but-uninitialized field, dead dispatch variant, non-exhaustive tag dispatch, signature mismatch — each diagnostic naming all three artifacts (struct, function, construction site) for single-hop repair; snapshot-vs-live is explicit and coercion-free via env-field types.
- Replacing metaprogramming: the closed constant sublanguage (target parameters included, so A007-style hardware baking works) + deterministic external generators under provenance checking (mechanism 2 above) + compiler-derived facts about constant data; if arrays-loops adopts a schedule layer it is declarative in-language structure with schedule SEARCH in the AI/tooling loop.
- Replacing exceptions: verifier-checked Result propagation riding D001 monomorphization; auto-derived propagation context/cause chains (zero hand-written tokens per site — this neutralizes the strongest pro-unwinding argument); a language-enforced Result-vs-trap classification rule; anti-sentinel lints; trap-with-report=abort with CFI-descriptive (non-executing) stack metadata; declared process-isolation doctrine so catch/recover cannot bolt on.
- Replacing module interfaces: function-level contract-bearing signatures (ownership, effects, aliasing, modes) re-verified against bodies on EVERY build as the redundant second statement of intent against coherently-hallucinated caller/callee pairs; compiler-GENERATED token-compact interface views on demand (zero semantic weight, never stored); content-addressed function-level memoized checking and dev-mode codegen for iteration latency; explicit fact-boundary declaration constructs (FFI extern regions, unsafe regions, reflection capability grants) as core surface since modules no longer house them.
- Replacing automatic memory management: writer-stated compiler-checked lifetime/region contracts on every allocation; compiler-derived drop points surfaced as explicit IR ops AND machine-readable per-function lifetime reports (F009 route — visible, never invisible); compiler-enforced canonical annotation spellings; lint-class region-quality and reclamation-timeliness diagnostics closing the two well-typed-but-wrong holes (one-big-arena pessimization, too-long-lived-region leaks).
- Replacing implicit conversions: explicit named conversion operations at every representation change; writer-stated inferable facts are legal and cross-checked against inference with mismatch as a hard error (redundancy as hallucination checksum).
- Replacing the interfaces bundle pending the pivot experiment: the contract-group kernel (named signature+law bundles, per-member conformance checking, provenance metadata) + C004-class capability predicates + the explicit closed-world dispatch construct — the machinery BOTH trial parties conceded is needed under any outcome, currently undesigned.

## Additions required because the writer is an AI

- Machine-readable diagnostics as a universal contract: node-path references into the canonical artifact; cross-file provenance chains for nonlocal closed-world errors (ownership violations surfacing far from cause); template-level aggregation so one defect yields one repair site, not N per-copy errors; deterministic local parse-error recovery with no heuristic cascades.
- Compiler-emitted canonical fully-elaborated form as the mandatory RE-READ artifact: every inferred interior type/mode/effect re-elaborated into it immediately; any AI workflow reading raw source without the elaborator is out of contract (this is a hard tooling requirement, not a convenience — the type-inference verdict's elision option is void without it).
- Checkable redundancy channels, since same-pass writer output is error-correlated with itself: optional writer-stated inferable facts cross-checked against inference (mismatch = hard error); function-level contracts as an independent second statement of intent; the interfaces experiment decides whether interior annotations join this set.
- Site-enumerated mechanical edit lists on every closed-set change (variant added, contract signature changed): file/site/missing-arm tuples an unattended writer can execute as a to-do list — the diagnostic form that makes O(K) propagation tractable for a fallible writer.
- Content-addressed caching of parse/typecheck/verification results on canonical bytes, converting the canonical-form decision into iteration-loop latency wins (compile time is an admissible AI cost channel, D001/D004).
- Contract-edit gating: editing a declared contract is a privileged, gated toolchain operation, so a writer cannot launder a hallucinated body through the checker by rewriting the declaration to match it (applies identically to signatures, law bundles, and lifetime contracts).
- Multi-process test/fuzz harness engineered around trap=abort (one diagnostic per process death; loss of warm in-process state) — the loop-economics cost the error-handling verdict knowingly accepted; wall-clock must be measured and acceptable or the checked-tier question reopens at the toolchain (never source) level.
- Machine-readable trap reports with stack attribution (the only carded post-violation observability story, C002-adjacent) powered by descriptive CFI metadata, doubling as the offline-profile channel for static-vs-profile.
- Lint-class machine-actionable feedback for well-typed-but-wrong states invisible to the type system: region-assignment quality, reclamation timeliness, in-band sentinel encoding, structural env-struct/table duplication reports.
- Toolchain-boundary policing: no pretty-printer, macro layer, or out-of-core dialect may introduce a second stored spelling; any future write-path codec must be bijective, version-pinned, parse-print-parse-fixpoint-checked, and echo only canonical form; violation semantics for provenance-check failures live in the build system.
- Generator/provenance infrastructure (mechanism 2 of the consistency layer) as a first-class toolchain component: deterministic external generators as source-of-record producers, provenance records ergonomic enough for an AI writer to emit reliably (itself EMPIRICAL-UNTESTED).

## Open tensions

- Generics vs interfaces on the SAME pivot: the generics verdict (keep_essential) puts a single generic body with declared, once-checked BOUNDS in the core; the interfaces verdict rules that exact question — bounded quantification vs concretized contract groups — needs_evidence and 'empirically unarbitrable today'. As written, generics has already decided interfaces' contested residue. Either the interfaces experiment is demoted to deciding only the CONTRACT-GROUP surface around the kept generics, or the two rulings must be formally reconciled — and generics' 'declared bounds' presuppose the contract-group kernel that interfaces marks undesigned. Who owns bounds is unassigned.
- Closures vs dynamic-dispatch on the escape valve: the closures verdict REQUIRES escaping/heterogeneous function values to route through 'the explicit, restricted D003-style dynamic construct' — a construct the dynamic-dispatch verdict simultaneously cut from core and remanded to the undesigned periphery. In core, only closed tag-enums/record-of-closures remain, and the closures verdict itself flags record-of-closures as a degenerate existential — 'the crack through which restricted dyn could legitimately re-enter'. Needs a joint ruling, not two independent ones.
- Modules' closed-world/no-ABI cut vs the generics sharing axis 'held open': D005 bounded sharing and the F009 Swift-style fourth model imply witness tables and stable ABI machinery whose entire rationale contradicts the modules verdict. Both verdicts record the coupling but point in opposite directions if the measurement program favors sharing. One of 'sharing stays open' and 'no internal ABI ever' will have to yield.
- The metaprogramming ruling arguably contradicts the generics COLLAPSE argument: generics ruled that a checker able to compute template[T] should BE the expander (generation-by-construction dominates check-after-generation), yet metaprogramming ruled the opposite architecture — external generation + provenance diff (check-after-generation) — for every pattern not parametric over types. The boundary 'types → in-language template; values/structure → external generator' is asserted, not derived; value-parametric pattern families sit ambiguously, and pressure there is exactly the workload-census reopening condition metaprogramming names.
- Trap=abort vs the AI iteration loop: error-handling accepts one-diagnostic-per-process-death and unexercisable failure-path cleanup as toolchain problems, while violation-semantics (research_needed) still owes a checked tier. If the multi-process harness measurement fails, the pressure lands on a dual-mode (checked-tier unwinding) design the error-handling verdict names as its own strongest standing objection — a genuine unresolved fork.
- Fully-elaborated stored artifact (syntax) vs interior elision (type-inference, open): if elision wins its experiment, the writer EMITS non-elaborated text but the language STORES only the elaborated canonical form — creating exactly the second-spelling / write-path-codec situation the syntax verdict polices. The elaborator then IS a codec and must meet the bijectivity/fixpoint rules; neither verdict says which artifact is the source of record during a repair loop.
- Compiler freedom vs predictability, unresolved charter question: memory-automation grants the compiler drop sink/batch/elide freedom, dispatch bans silent devirtualization-dependence, and generics flags 'may the compiler silently de-duplicate/devirtualize what the writer wrote explicitly' as the open performance-transparency question (predictable cost model vs guaranteed-optimization floor). Three verdicts assume different answers; it gates all of them and is assigned to no one.
- Everything remanded to the periphery, and the periphery doesn't exist: open-set dispatch, foreign unwinds, dynamic loading, reflection capabilities, and all fact attenuation now live at a single FFI wall whose design has zero primary-source cards (ffi_abi_runtime: missing_primary_source, priority-high). The periphery is accreting into a second, evidence-free language; multiple verdicts list its design as a soundness PRECONDITION while none blocks on it procedurally.
- Consistency-layer proliferation vs coherence: six verdicts each mandated their own drift mechanism (provenance diff, one-definition tables, env-struct dedup, elaboration diffing, contract groups, canonical spellings). This synthesis unifies them over the canonical artifact, but no verdict authorizes that unification — if they ship as six separate subsystems, the toolchain reproduces the very near-duplicate-machinery drift the charter forbids in source.
- Load concentration on provisional evidence: checked ownership (aliasing round 2 not yet run) and card D001 are single points of failure for memory-automation, closures, modules, error-handling, and generics simultaneously; C004 — now known to be provisional, not confirmed — is load-bearing for the interfaces kernel and concurrency coupling. The verdict lattice is coherent but rests on a handful of unverified cards the standing rule says must survive adversarial verification before any of this hardens into spec.
