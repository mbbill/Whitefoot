# Round 4 (v2, corrected D2) — Familiarity vs regularity (`familiarity`)

**Outcome:** `research_needed`

**Leading candidate:** Amended Position 3 (lexicon/grammar split): the round-2 alien-regular canonical grammar stands untouched (uniform node shape — Position 1's Rust-shaped node inventory stays out unless the three-arm experiment shows a large, persistent penalty for the uniform shape); the lexicon is selectively borrowed from the Rust prior ONLY where a completed divergence census shows substantial semantic match, with fully self-contained spec definitions (familiar names as mnemonics only — no definition-by-delta, no load-bearing 'differs from Rust' lines), minted novel tokens at every divergence point (numeric-mode vocabulary explicitly minted, not borrowed: Rust's unchecked_* is UB where ours is poison), and one written borrow-vs-mint policy stated once with a named owner.

## Rationale

The critique collapses the debate to something much narrower than the question as posed. Grammar regularity is not actually at stake: round-2 canonical form (one spelling, no sugar, no precedence, no elision, byte-canonical, tree-isomorphic) is decided law, Position 1 operates inside it, and Position 2's own steelman admits rule-free familiar lexemes — so the live variables are only (1) node-shape inventory and (2) lexicon policy. On (1), Position 1's distinctive claim survives only as an untested behavioral bet: its spec-neutrality headline contradicts its own concession, its dominance argument is empirical not structural (parenthesization and mode qualifiers are still re-encoding steps), and its central experiment arm is unfiled — while Position 2's reversibility argument survives critique intact: the parked gated codec makes familiarity purchasable later, whereas Rust-shaped canonical grammar is a language break to undo. On (2), Position 3 correctly prices the terrain (lexicon is first-order spec-neutral; the spec's own examples sit inside the protected D2 budget; much of the vocabulary names semantics that are Rust's by decree), but its showcase contains a confirmed false friend and its census is unrun, so borrowing must be census-gated and self-contained. Position 2's loud/silent asymmetry — its claimed escape from the empirical deadlock — fails on the record's own text: both surfaces own a named verifier-invisible silent class, and the decided floors partially defuse both flagship exhibits. What actually decides the residue is the relative rate of two unmeasured silent error classes plus the penalty-decay slope and example-budget differential — pure model behavior with zero cards — hence research_needed. The amended split is the leading candidate because it is the only position robust to the experiments' outcome: if familiarity proves worthless, the cost was a small de-alias table inside an unchanged grammar; if it proves valuable, nearly all of the harvestable benefit (lexicon plus spec-example compactness) is already captured without the irreversible grammar-shape bet. Two non-empirical items must precede any hardening: the owner ruling fixing D2's unit of measure, and the four computable-now censuses that make the model experiments interpretable.

**Strengths:** Correctly dissolves the debate's false dichotomy along the load-bearing seam: regularity lives in the grammar (already decided law — round2-syntax; A001's negative scope leaves lexicon genuinely undecided), familiarity lives in the token strings, and lexicon choice is spec-budget-neutral to first order. **Weaknesses:** The critique lands three real hits: (1) internal contradiction — if spec definitions are fully self-contained, the 'differs from Rust' delta lines are redundant mass; if they are load-bearing, the spec is conditioned on an unverified drifting prior (definition-by-delta, which its own concession disclaims); it must pick self-contained-plus-mnemonics and forfeit the delta lines' claimed function. (2) Its showcase contains an actual false friend: Rust's unchecked_* arithmetic is UB-on-overflow while the corresponding promise here is nsw/nuw-POISON, a distinction N001's ladder makes load-bearing — the 'pre-stabilized vocabulary' can import a semantic blur at exactly a divergence point, and the round2-type-inference line 30 'pre-stabilized' gloss confuses fixed strings with stable name-to-semantics mappings. 'For most of the vocabulary the Rust name is the TRUE name' rests on a divergence census that has never been run, and the conceded residual silent channel (wrong-but-legal token selection under prior habit) is precisely the class the strict grammar cannot catch.

## Key tradeoffs

- D2 unit of measure (the sharpest tension, correctly identified by the critic): under a rule-count/irregularity reading (user-directives line 18) Position 2 wins by definition before any experiment; under a total in-context teaching-footprint reading (line 16 places 'examples' inside the description that must be small) Positions 1/3 can win — but only via 100% EMPIRICAL-UNTESTED example-efficiency claims. Both readings are textually supported; no one has asked the owner to fix the unit, and the positions are incommensurable until it is fixed.
- Node-shape inventory (the only grammar question actually live): Rust-shaped forms buy an untested prior-transfer benefit — bounded above by the skeleton's share of tokens in a round-3-envelope elaborated artifact, which nobody has measured — at a spec-mass cost that is computable today by drafting both grammars and counting productions.
- Lexicon borrowing: buys real BPE compactness of the spec's own in-context examples plus true-name alignment for semantics that are Rust's by decree, at the price of a de-alias table proportional to an unrun divergence census plus the one silent channel the canonical grammar cannot convert to a hard error (wrong-but-legal token selection at divergence points, e.g. unchecked-UB vs poison).
- Silent-error symmetry: each surface owns a record-named verifier-invisible typechecks-but-wrong class — alien: transcription-distance operand-order/associativity swaps (round2-syntax); familiar: prior-import token selection — while the decided floors (per-node modes, canonical single spelling, grammar-rejected unlicensed forms) partially defuse BOTH flagship exhibits. The decisive quantity is the relative rate of two unmeasured silent classes, and the measurement has a shelf life across model generations shorter than the irreversibility of the decision it settles.
- Reversibility asymmetry: an alien-regular canonical core keeps familiarity purchasable later as the parked gated bijective codec (tooling, zero spec cost); baking familiar grammar shapes into the stored canonical form is a language break to undo after artifacts and teaching examples accumulate. Lexicon choice, however, is sticky in BOTH directions (renaming is a breaking gated vocabulary event), so the codec escape hatch de-risks the grammar-shape decision far more than the lexicon decision.
- Token-frequency benefit and autocomplete-contamination hazard are the same coin: the tokenizer statistics that make 'fn'/'match' cheap single tokens are what make Rust continuations mechanically high-probability after them — no brief weighed these jointly; the experiments must measure them on the same arm.
- Falsifiability asymmetry (critic's most consequential addition): with a familiar lexicon, a passing teachability eval cannot distinguish spec-driven from prior-driven competence — 'the spec+examples suffice' is testable only on the alien rendering. Spec bugs masked by prior compensation surface only when the prior shifts; this is a long-term assurance cost of familiarity no position priced.

## Evidence gaps

- Zero AI-codegen evidence cards (round-2 standing flag; caveats.md standing rule): every prior-transfer, penalty-decay, false-friend, contamination, and example-budget claim in all three briefs is EMPIRICAL-UNTESTED.
- The filed Syntax A/B (missing-research-backlog.jsonl line 43 — verified this session) is two-arm, canonical vs infix; the familiar-lexeme-canonical arm that Position 1's and Position 3's cases ride on exists in no backlog item, and no pre-committed kill thresholds for it exist anywhere.
- Four computable-now, model-free artifacts have never been produced: (a) the spec-mass arithmetic (draft both grammar specs, count productions and exception clauses); (b) the real-BPE tokenizer census E3 (the 1.35-1.48x figure is a flagged regex proxy on fragments); (c) the divergence census (which Rust names are true names vs false friends — prerequisite for placing the false-friend battery's traps); (d) the skeleton-share census (fraction of tokens in a representative round-3-envelope elaborated artifact covered by any Rust prior — upper-bounds the familiarity benefit).
- D009 and D010 remain direct-extraction with no adversarial-verification votes (verified: round3-synthesis no-votes list; the 2026-07-02 log covers only D001 and C004), yet both were deployed load-bearing without flags — one citation-discipline violation per side.
- Neither silent error class has a measured rate: alien transcription-distance swaps and familiar prior-import token selection are both hypothesized only; no differential-testing data, no mixed-dialect (uncanny valley) data, no penalty-decay curves per model generation.
- The owner has not ruled on the D2 unit of measure (rule count only vs total spec+examples in-context footprint) — a directive-interpretation gap, not an empirical one, and the single item that determines which position's arithmetic is even admissible.
- Repair-loop exposure effects are unmodeled: every diagnostic/edit-list iteration feeds the model more canonical-form text, so production penalty decay may be dominated by session-accumulated exposure rather than curated examples — this confounds both the persistent-tax risk and the pre-committed kill conditions unless the experiment controls for it.
- The marginal value of surface familiarity CONDITIONAL on the decided tooling architecture (node-path machine diagnostics, site-enumerated edit lists, elaborated re-read views) is a different quantity from raw generation familiarity and is the one that matters here; no experiment as specified isolates it.
- The elaborator-as-codec / source-of-record tension (round2-synthesis open tension; round2-type-inference) is unresolved: if interior elision wins its experiment, the write surface and re-read surface diverge, and familiarity/regularity could be split across them — all three positions assumed one surface serves both directions.
