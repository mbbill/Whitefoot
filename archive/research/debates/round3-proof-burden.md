# Round 3 — AI writer proof burden (`proof-burden`)

**Outcome:** `design_candidate_later`

**Leading candidate:** Amended Rust-division (P2) as the mandatory floor, with an elastic checked channel above it. Mandatory writer burden = exactly the round-2 decided floors and nothing more: intent facts (ownership/aliasing capability, effect row, numeric mode, ABI-visible layout) at every signature/interface/instantiation, plus writer-stated storage/lifetime contracts at allocations; the compiler carries all derivation; nothing writer-stated is ever trusted unchecked. Three amendments forced by the critique: (1) P2's 'statement never creates facts' claim is struck — a stated-and-checked writer channel for checker-underivable facts (loop invariants, ranges, disjointness, law obligations) is retained as OPTIONAL, per-fact-class, pending the extended redundancy-independence and proof-ladder experiments; (2) a deterministically-budgeted solver pass is admissible only as a fact-PROMOTION stage that never gates program acceptance and never elides safety-relevant checks until a proof-checker TCB design exists (P3 absorbed with its third-state hint hole to be legislated); (3) proof density beyond the floor is a tunable coverage/performance parameter, not a trust decision — P1 survives only as the maximal setting of the optional channel and is ineligible as a mandate (metaprogramming-law violation as written, non-canonicalizable proof surface, and D2's amended verbosity budget).

## Rationale

Decisive where law forces it: D1 plus the verification spine settle the trust question completely — no writer-stated fact is ever load-bearing unchecked, so proof burden is not a soundness dial in any position, and the mandatory writer surface today can only be the already-decided floors, because mandating more requires writer-behavior evidence the corpus states it has zero of. That skeleton is P2, the only regime whose every mechanism has adversarially-verified existence proofs (F001 3-0; C004 and D001 now CONFIRMED per the 2026-07-02 verification file, which I inspected — the newly-verified cards strengthen rather than reverse the leader, distinguishing this from round-1 violation-semantics' research_needed posture), and the only one forward-compatible with every experiment outcome. But P2 cannot stand unamended: the critic's sharpest point is verified against the spine's own text — 'stated-and-checked' is fact-creating for checker-underivable facts, so P2's decidability cap is a permanent D0 performance forfeiture it never prices, and the highest-value optimizer facts live exactly there. P1's density and P3's solver are therefore retained as the elastic, optional, always-checked layer above the floor — P1 is barred as a mandate on three independent grounds (it violates the decided compiler-derived-constant-facts law as literally stated; a proof surface is non-canonicalizable, colliding with one-spelling-per-construct and content-addressed caching; and D2, dated 2026-07-02 in user-directives.md, amends the 'verbosity is free' premise this debate was framed on, converting its token-cost-zero economics into a directive-law violation no party briefed). P3's lattice is architecturally attractive and D2-friendly (compute, not context) but design-incomplete at two soundness-adjacent points (the check-elision/TCB contradiction and the undischarged-hint third state), so it enters as a fenced promotion stage, not a regime. Honest where evidence is thin: whether the optional channel becomes mandatory per fact class, how much the solver actually discharges, and whether an AI writer can supply useful checked invariants at all are the deciding variables, and they have literally zero cards — hence design_candidate_later, not a decision, with the choice of failure currency under unknown writer error-rate (recoverable iterations vs permanent forfeiture vs TCB growth) explicitly deferred to the experiment program.

## Position assessments (post-critique)

### P1 — Maximal proof burden: proof-carrying source (writer emits SPARK-grade checked annotations everywhere; compiler is checker-only, never infers)

**Strengths:** Its core insight survives the critique and refutes P2's dissolution move: for checker-underivable facts (loop invariants, value-dependent disjointness, trip counts, ranges — exactly A005's consumer list), writer statement is fact-CREATING, the only route past the decidability cap, because verification is cheaper than synthesis; the spine's stated-and-checked arm exists for precisely this. Its soundness is invariant to the unknown writer error-rate ε — false facts are compile errors, never miscompiles — so its failure currency (repair iterations) is recoverable where P2's (forfeited facts) is not. It moves nearly all fact mass into the one tier all round-1 positions agreed is assumable in every build mode. Concessions were the most honest in the debate (weak-annotation equilibrium, checker-TCB growth, one-way door).

**Weaknesses:** Four landed blows, two fatal to the position as written. (1) The 'compiler never infers optimizer-consumable facts' pillar violates decided law, not an amendable edge: round2-metaprogramming/synthesis mandates 'facts about constant data are compiler-derived by inspection, never writer-asserted' — the opposite polarity for an entire fact class. (2) The canonical-form conflict is structural and unrebutted: invariant equivalence is undecidable, so 'one spelling per semantic construct' cannot extend to a proof surface; equivalent-but-differently-spelled invariants are distinct canonical bytes, breaking drift-diffing and content-addressed caching under regeneration-heavy writers. (3) Its economic premise 'every fact stated costs only tokens, priced at zero' is now contrary to directive law: D2 (2026-07-02) amends 'verbosity is free' — proof-carrying source maximizes both SPEC budget (an invariant/VC vocabulary must be taught in-context to a model with zero training data) and PROGRAM budget; no party briefed this. (4) Proof-of-consistency is not proof-of-intent: a dischargeability-optimizing writer selects invariants provable from the same plan that produced the code, making weakening-laundering the plausible equilibrium, so the claimed fact-density gain is an unproven upper bound purchased with the largest one-way door. Mechanism family has zero corpus cards (no Dafny/Why3/F*/PCC), and deterministic resource-bounded discharge is undesigned. Survives only as the maximal setting of an optional checked channel, not as a mandate.

### P2 — Rust-division: writer states intent at boundaries (decided floors); compiler carries the entire derivation burden; writer-stated facts beyond the floor are optional checked redundancy, never trusted

**Strengths:** It is the fixed point of decided law: boundary-explicit intent, zero cross-boundary inference, deterministic ambiguity-rejecting interior synthesis, optional cross-checked redundancy with mismatch-as-hard-error, and contract-edit gating are all already round-2 floors — this position adds nothing and removes nothing. It is the only regime whose every load-bearing mechanism has adversarially-verified existence proofs at the D1 bar, and the 2026-07-02 verification file STRENGTHENS it beyond its own brief: C004 is now CONFIRMED_WITH_AMENDED_SOURCES and D001 CONFIRMED (the position understated its own evidence). Deterministic accept/reject preserves content-addressed memoization and the machine repair loop. Post-D2 it has a structural advantage no one argued: compiler-carried burden spends compute, not context budget. It is forward-compatible with every experimental outcome — if redundancy-independence mandates interior annotations they move from optional to mandatory channel without re-architecture; a solver pass can be added later as a fact-promotion stage without changing the writer contract.

**Weaknesses:** Its central dissolution move — 'writer proof burden can NEVER buy trust, only redundancy' — is refuted for checker-underivable facts, where a statement is the enabling input that converts an underivable fact into a checkable one; the position therefore caps the reachable fact set at deterministic-analysis decidability, a PERMANENT performance forfeiture under D0's extreme-performance mandate that it prices at zero (conceding only 'restructure loops'). Its laundering argument is a category error (interior invariant weakening is strength-erosion, a performance event, not the soundness-laundering gating exists to stop). Its determinism indictment attacks prove-or-reject (F006-style), not P3's actual prove-or-check-or-omit design. The boundary-independence claim doing the mandatory-vs-optional work (caller/callee generated in different contexts) is itself an exempted EMPIRICAL-UNTESTED writer-behavior claim. D009 — its silent-degradation exhibit — remains genuinely unverified, and the effect-row leg of its intent floor has zero exemplar cards (Cyclone/MLKit/Koka/DPJ, a named blocking obligation). Frontier size past borrow-checkability is unmeasured and is the decisive variable.

### P3 — Solver-assisted obligations: writer states only the floor facts plus optional checked hints; compiler generates residual obligations; a budgeted deterministic prover promotes what it discharges; residue falls to runtime-check or sound omission

**Strengths:** Correctly reframes burden as an elastic dial that is sound at every setting: budget exhaustion moves an obligation from proven to checked to omitted — always a performance event, never a soundness event — and every carded fact channel has a sound omission fallback (N002 unflagged, N004, N005 non-inbounds). Its tier-3 fallback matches round-1 violation-semantics' amended leading candidate (A005 runtime checks + versioning), and it is the only position with a post-violation observability story (C002 precedent under trap=abort). Routing burden to toolchain compute is the one cost channel that remains cleanly admissible even after D2 (CPU, not context tokens). Its hint channel generalizes the already-decided redundancy-as-hallucination-checksum machinery, and hints, being solver-checked, can enlarge but never poison the proven set.

**Weaknesses:** Three design holes, one internal contradiction. (1) 'The solver never touches the D1 envelope' contradicts its own payoff claim: if solver-discharged facts ever elide safety-relevant runtime checks (the check-elision payoff it prices), a solver soundness bug is a D1-class breach — the architectural fence is asserted, undesigned, and incompatible with the payoff; either forfeit elision or admit the solver into the D1 trust path. (2) The undischarged-hint semantics create exactly the spine-forbidden third state or nondeterministic acceptance across solver versions — breaking canonical determinism and memoization — and the position never legislates which. (3) Its distinctive tier has zero corpus cards: F006 evidences prover-enforced RESTRICTION (decidability bought by bans), not budgeted SMT discharge of arithmetic/disjointness obligations, and the single number its coverage story depends on (SPARK discharge rates) is listed as missing/blocking. 'Prove-or-check-or-omit exhausts the sound design space' overclaims (the offline-profile channel is alive; A005 is itself static speculation-with-fallback). TCB enlargement runs against the metaprogramming verdict's confirmed restriction pattern, and performance nondeterminism collides with the unresolved predictable-cost-model charter question.

## Key tradeoffs

- Fact-creation vs error-surface at the high-value frontier (the P1/P2 head-on collision the decided law does not settle): writer statement is the only route past checker decidability to the top-payoff facts (dependence-freedom, disjointness, trip counts, purity), but statement quality under a correlated, dischargeability-optimizing writer is unproven — 'stated-and-checked' is simultaneously the channel P1 maximizes and P2 caps at boundaries, and the verification spine blesses both readings.
- Failure-currency choice under unknown writer error-rate ε: all three regimes are D1-sound by construction, so proof burden is the dial that selects WHICH currency is paid — recoverable compile-loop explosion (P1), permanent conservative performance forfeiture (P2), or trusted-computing-base growth plus nondeterministic performance (P3) — and the corpus cannot rank these currencies today.
- Deterministic acceptance vs discharge power: borrow-checking-class analysis gives stable accept/reject, machine-actionable blame, and memoizable checking; solver assistance widens fact coverage but must be fenced out of the acceptance decision (fact-promotion only) or budget nondeterminism breaks canonical determinism — the hint channel is where this fence currently fails.
- Canonicalizability of the proof surface: invariants have unboundedly many logically-equivalent spellings and equivalence is undecidable, so any mandatory proof-carrying surface conflicts with one-spelling-per-construct, drift-diffing, and content-addressed caching; proof density beyond the floor must live outside the byte-canonical source or under new undesigned spelling rules.
- Post-D2 budget repricing (unbriefed by all parties): verbosity is no longer free — writer-carried proof burden spends finite SPEC budget (teaching the annotation vocabulary in-context) and PROGRAM budget (emitting it per code unit), while compiler/solver-carried burden spends admissible compute; D2 structurally favors compiler-carried derivation and makes maximal mandatory annotation density a directive-law problem, not just an unmeasured cost.
- Uniform regime vs per-fact-class decomposition: bounds/overflow (per-operation checkable), loop-bounded disjointness (runtime-checkable via A005 only), whole-execution noalias and purity/laws (static-only evidence, no dynamic check) differ in derivability, statability, and checkability — round-1 already warned one policy per contract class may be a category error, and a per-class burden table is the likely true shape of the answer.
- Monotone coupling with the escape-hatch sibling: higher mandatory burden, higher conservative-rejection rates, or budget cliffs all raise pressure into the unsafe/FFI region; deciding proof burden and escape-hatch friction independently can produce the worst composite (maximal burden + cheap escape reverses the intended fact-density gain).

- D2 budget pricing (new gap, post-directive): the SPEC-budget cost of teaching each annotation-vocabulary tier in-context and the PROGRAM-budget cost per burden level are unmeasured; no position's economics were computed against the amended verbosity law.
- FFI fact-attenuation remains undesigned while the spine taken literally forbids attenuated facts (neither stated-and-checked nor checker-derived) — the proof-burden ruling must legislate the exception class, and every position's 'shrinks toward the FFI wall' rhetoric is unpriced until it does.
