# Round 2 — Macros / comptime / metaprogramming (`metaprogramming`)

**Verdict:** `replace_with_alternative`

## Rationale

The null hypothesis survives, but the naked alternative does not — hence replace_with_alternative. (1) The keep is undischargeable today: the corpus contains zero macro/comptime/staging/CTFE evidence (verified by full-text search across all notes, debates, synthesis, and implementation files; the missing-research backlog does not list it), and caveats.md's standing rule requires exact card citation. The defense honestly conceded its case is 'a design candidate argument, not a carded conclusion' and surrendered syntactic/token/AST macros outright, so keep_essential is unavailable. (2) The defense's most powerful material — preserving parametric definitions so the specialize-vs-share axis stays a compiler decision (D001/D005, F009 fourth model, all verified against round1-dispatch-generics.md) — is genuinely strong but belongs to the dispatch-generics feature, which already keeps typed in-compiler instantiation as its leading candidate. Judged within its own remit, metaprogramming's unique load reduces to comptime value computation, derive-style generation, and cross-copy consistency. (3) For that residue, the corpus's only confirmed directional evidence favors restriction over evaluation: F006's true-by-construction-via-restriction pattern, F009's facts-below-source lesson, and the ir-strategy round's disqualification of uncheckable trusted contracts all argue against embedding a Turing-complete evaluator that must bit-match the N001/N002 numeric contract inside a compiler whose charter is verifiable simplicity. (4) But the prosecution's own concessions establish that a bare cut is incoherent: constant expressions must exist anyway, and the defense proved two rate-independent verification gaps (undetectable cross-copy drift; writer-asserted constant facts feeding UB channels). These are closable only if the alternative is promoted from option to obligation — deterministic external generation with build-enforced provenance diffing, and compiler-derived (never writer-asserted) constant facts. The verdict therefore cuts user-visible staging from the core and binds the language and toolchain to the eight requirements listed. All AI-writer behavioral claims on both sides remain EMPIRICAL-UNTESTED; they set the follow-up program and the reopening conditions, not the ruling, because the structural case does not hinge on them.

## Optimizer role

None that is unique to the feature. By optimization time, macro-expanded and AI-pre-expanded code are indistinguishable inputs (F002: the mechanism depends on proven non-interference, not on how text was produced), so in-language expansion contributes zero to the extreme-performance goal per se. The one genuine optimizer-necessity argument the defense raised — preserving parametric structure so the specialize-vs-share decision stays in-compiler (D001 vs D005, and the F009/Swift-SIL fourth model the dispatch round flagged as unexamined) — is real and verified against round1-dispatch-generics.md, but it is carried by the typed-generics feature adjudicated in that topic, not by macros/comptime; the defender conceded this coupling explicitly. Comptime-derived constants feeding fact channels (N004 range, F003 noalias, N005 inbounds) is the residual optimizer claim, and it is answerable without staging: facts about literal constant data can be compiler-DERIVED by inspection, which is equally sound and requires no evaluator. A006 (Halide) cuts prosecution's way at card level: optimization choices as checkable program STRUCTURE the writer can emit directly, with a negative scope denying transfer of the staged-DSL machinery to a general language.

## Verification role

This is where the case was actually decided, and it cuts both ways. Against keeping: an in-language Turing-complete evaluator enlarges the trusted surface in exactly the direction the corpus's confirmed pattern forbids — F006 (SPARK) achieves true-by-construction facts by RESTRICTING semantics to a side-effect-free fragment; the ir-strategy verdict's non-negotiable soundness rule disqualifies uncheckable trusted contracts; a CTFE stage must bit-match the treacherous runtime numeric contract (N001 UB/poison ladder, N002 flag semantics) or become a cross-stage miscompile generator; and generator verification forces either late per-instantiation checking or a quantified staged type system with no supporting card. Zero staging keeps every obligation first-order, per-site, decidable. Against a bare cut: the defense proved two structural verification gaps in the naked alternative — (a) cross-copy consistency ('these N functions instantiate one pattern') is stated in no artifact, so no in-language checker can exist at ANY drift rate; (b) AI-precomputed constants asserted into UB-bearing channels are exactly the writer-trusted contracts the ir-strategy round disqualified. Both gaps are closable by requirements outside the trusted compiler (provenance-checked deterministic generation; compiler-derived-not-writer-asserted constant facts), which is why the verdict is replace_with_alternative rather than cut_from_core: the alternative carries the load only if those obligations are made mandatory.

## AI-writer considerations

All admissible machine-writer claims on both sides are EMPIRICAL-UNTESTED — the corpus contains zero AI-codegen evidence cards and zero metaprogramming cards of any kind (verified by full-corpus search). Conceded-real costs of the alternative: O(N)-copy token generation and re-read on every pattern edit vs O(1) definition edits; consistency drift across near-duplicates (the strongest defense card — and its structural point stands: without a pattern artifact, drift is undetectable in-language at any rate); N-fold diagnostics without expansion provenance; update amplification moving regeneration latency into the AI loop; per-copy contract restatement multiplying proof obligations. Conceded-real costs of keeping: unbounded (even fuel-bounded) CTFE makes compile time a hazard inside the AI iteration loop (D001/D004/D009 negative scopes flag specialization compile-time costs, quantified nowhere per caveats.md); a second evaluation semantics the writer must model creates a new error class (defender's honest concession); expansion-backtrace diagnostics point into code that exists in no file, whereas literal-source diagnostics point at text the writer actually wrote — the prosecution's repair-loop asymmetry claim is plausible but untested. The verdict routes the writer-cost burden to mandatory toolchain requirements (deterministic external generators as source-of-record, provenance records, diagnostic aggregation to pattern-level repair sites) rather than into the trusted compiler.

## What must be true

- The language MUST include a closed, decidable, trivially-terminating constant-expression sublanguage (built-in arithmetic/layout/target-parameter constants only; NO user-defined function evaluation at compile time, hence no fuel, no hygiene, no cross-stage name resolution) so that array sizes, layout constants (A001-adjacent), and numeric-mode constants remain statable. 'Zero staging' is hereby defined as: no user-visible compile-time EXECUTION semantics and no code-producing stages — not 'no constant folding'.
- Optimizer facts about constant data MUST be compiler-derived by inspection of literals (ranges, alignment, sizes) or compiler-checked against stated contracts before emission into UB-bearing channels (F003 noalias, N002 nsw/nuw, N004 range, N005 inbounds). Writer-asserted facts about constants are forbidden — this closes the defense's hallucinated-table miscompile channel and satisfies the ir-strategy round's disqualification of uncheckable trusted contracts.
- Consistency-checking of repeated code MUST exist as a toolchain requirement: intended-identical or pattern-instantiated code regions carry machine-readable provenance (generator identity, canonical inputs, content hash); the build re-runs deterministic external generators and diffs their output against the source of record, failing the build on divergence. This is the drift detector the defense correctly showed cannot exist in-language under zero staging — it is relocated outside the trusted compiler, not waived.
- The generics boundary is drawn crisply: parametric, type-checked-before-instantiation generics with in-compiler instantiation remain the sanctioned single-definition mechanism under the dispatch-generics candidate (D001/D002/D003), and this cut MUST NOT be read as mandating AI-side monomorphization or foreclosing the explicitly-open D005 bounded-sharing / F009 fourth-model axis — that decision belongs to the dispatch-generics topic and its ordered measurement program.
- Target parameterization (vector widths, cache-line sizes, cfg variants) MUST be expressible through the constant sublanguage's target-parameter constants or through per-target provenance-checked generation, so A007-style hardware baking does not multiply unverifiable source variants.
- If the arrays-loops prototype adopts a schedule layer (A006), it MUST be declarative structure interpreted by the compiler, with schedule SEARCH and autotuning living in the AI/tooling loop and landing as concrete checked structure; no in-language schedule generation. This reconciles the zero-staging ruling with round1-arrays-loops.md's leading candidate.
- Diagnostics MUST be machine-readable and point at literal source; provenance records must let tooling aggregate N copy-local diagnostics for a shared defect into one pattern-level repair site, answering the diagnostics-multiplication cost.
- Any future derive/annotation-processor or 'just one small macro' proposal MUST be adjudicated as external tooling against this verdict, not admitted as a core feature; violation semantics for provenance-check failures slot into the build system, not the graduated-obligations framework (which remains research_needed).
