# Static-basis derivability and cost hostile review

Status: D14 adversarial accounting review, 2026-07-15. Review target:
`STATIC-BASIS-DERIVABILITY-COST-PACKET.md`.

## Verdict

**PASS as a complete routing and falsification packet; FAIL-CLOSED for exact
derivability, structural parity, measurement, and production.**

The packet terminates every finite domain and capability-obligation category,
constructs representative ordinary-library routes, preserves protected weaker
shapes, and names unresolved exact leaves. It does not convert paper routes
into exact D-2/P-1 credit.

## Cost-field completeness

For the representative routes, the packet records or explicitly defers:

- asymptotic time and space;
- backing-allocation topology and committing growth;
- payload layout and contiguity;
- initialization and zero-fill traffic;
- copies, moves, relocation, and drop traffic;
- representation-selected metadata;
- indirection and dynamic dispatch;
- machine/OS event multiplicity;
- cleanup and recoverable-failure state;
- facts and invalidation; and
- absence of tax on protected weaker shapes.

Exact constants and several per-member branches remain in the existing family
locks and dense ledgers. The packet does not supersede them.

## Completion boundary

The research requested by D14 can now be returned to the owner with four
separate decisions: static gate selection, abstract basis acceptance for
further research, authorization of any bounded experiment, and later
production work. The packet supports the first two decisions and defines three
possible bounded experiments; it does not support production authorization.
