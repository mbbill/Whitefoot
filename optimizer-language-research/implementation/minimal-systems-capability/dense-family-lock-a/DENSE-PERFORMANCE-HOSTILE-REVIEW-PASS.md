## 4. Randomization, stopping, and exact power law

720 fresh-child observations per cell and target. It cannot stop, extend,
replace, or rerun adaptively. Only after its complete artifacts and memory

cell/target/cycle/salt held fixed across six Williams rows, and a full-field
global block-order grammar. The reviewer independently re-encoded every root,
permutation-manifest, block-identity, and block-order test vector. The six
Williams sequences place every treatment once in every period and contain each
ordered distinct predecessor pair exactly once.

power engine records `u[j,s,a,b]` in 0 through 6. It then forms
`Q[j,s](z)`, `P[j](z)=product_s Q[j,s](z)`, and
`M(z)=sum_j P[j](z)`. For `r=n/30`, the exact law is
`M(z)^r/(4*30^5)^r`. The reviewer reproduced the two dependence sentinels:
