# Round 2 — Interfaces/traits as abstraction (`interfaces`)

**Verdict:** `needs_evidence`

## Rationale

The trial decomposes cleanly, and the evidence decides the edges but not the pivot. Decided for the prosecution: interface conformance has zero attested optimizer value (no card shows any pass consuming an implements-fact; A005/F001-F003/A002/A010 all attach to concrete code), the dynamic path is a carded defeat with only conditional recovery (D002, D007, D008), and the trait rule-surface (D003 dyn-compatibility, coherence, bound solving) is verifier burden that discharges no optimizer-consumed fact. Decided for the defense: the named alternative as stated cannot carry the load — annotations on concrete functions only cannot express C004-class per-type capability predicates the round1 concurrency candidate requires, cannot state the cross-copy invariant whose absence turns regeneration drift into F003/N002-class miscompilation, and leaves the round1-dispatch candidate's explicit dyn construct without a domain; the prosecution itself concedes provenance-grouped compiler-checked cross-copy obligations, which reconstructs the contract-group half of interfaces under another name. Hence plain cut_from_core fails (the alternative does not carry the load as named) and keep_essential fails (necessity unproven: the optimizer case for keeping is option-preservation, not attested consumption; the verification case — the strongest single argument heard — hinges on the unresolved checked-vs-trusted status of law annotations, concedes site-count-not-kind, and partly rests on a mis-graded citation, C004 being provisional rather than workflow-confirmed). The genuinely contested residue is narrow: bounded quantification — whether a single generic body with guaranteed specialization lives in the core, or writers concretize into checked contract groups. That pivot is exactly the axis round1-dispatch ruled empirically unarbitrable (zero quantitative cards; the F009 fourth model unexamined; the F003-discharge constructive check never run), now compounded by AI-writer unknowns on which the corpus has zero evidence (drift rates, token magnitudes, repair-loop dynamics). Reversibility does not break the tie: sugar can be added later, but concretization erases family identity from the artifact and permanently forecloses the D005/F009 levers. Under the caveats.md standing rule and the corpus null-hypothesis discipline, ruling the pivot today would be a values commitment dressed as an evidence-driven one. Verdict: needs_evidence — with the decided sub-parts binding as recorded constraints (no implicit dynamic dispatch in core under any outcome; contract-group kernel, capability predicates, and explicit closed-world dispatch required under any outcome) and the deciding experiments named in followUps.

## Optimizer role

No carded pass consumes "T implements I" as an optimization fact: what backends attestedly eat is concrete-code facts — alias/non-interference (F001, F002, F003), reductions/inductions/FP-flags/loop-shape (A002, A005, A010), purity/effects (F006). The prosecution wins this sub-point cleanly. Interfaces' optimizer role is therefore indirect on both paths: the static path elaborates to exactly the concrete monomorphized functions a writer could emit directly (D001), and the dynamic path is a carded defeat (D002 vtable/no-inlining) with only conditional, revertible, option-dependent recovery (D007, D008); Julia evidence makes concreteness, not declared abstraction, the performance-bearing property (D009, D010). The real optimizer stakes are option-shaped, not conformance-shaped: a generic body present in the artifact is the prerequisite for compiler-side instantiation dedup, D005-style bounded sharing, and the F009/Swift-SIL model of optimizer-decided specialization — levers writer-side concretization forecloses permanently — and a language-known explicit dispatch construct prevents hand-rolled fn-pointer erasure, the D009 call-target-hiding defeat that round1-dispatch (A1 weakness #4) explicitly predicts when no sanctioned path exists. C004-class capability predicates (Send/Sync) let the optimizer treat non-atomic memory as unshared. Net: conformance-as-fact has zero attested optimizer value; implicit dynamic dispatch is net-negative and no one defends it; artifact-level generic structure has real but unmeasured option value on an axis round1 ruled empirically unarbitrable.

## Verification role

The strongest surviving argument in the trial is verificational and favors some grouping construct. Law annotations are UB-bearing optimizer contracts (F003; N002 "contracts, not mere annotations"; misleading-comparisons note 5), sound only if writer obligations are true (F001 negative scope). Writer-side concretization multiplies the independently-trusted assertion surface by the duplication factor and makes the invariant "these N functions implement one contract" inexpressible — post-hoc equivalence of drifted copies is undecidable. A contract group restores checkability (one contract site, one checked conformance per member); bounds additionally convert algorithm preconditions into compiler-checked obligations; a single generic body makes cross-copy body identity true by construction. Three caps: (a) the defense concedes the generic-body advantage over group-checked copies is site count plus by-construction identity, not a difference in kind — an impl can drift from its contract exactly as a copy can drift from its annotation; (b) the argument's weight scales with the unresolved checked-vs-trusted status of law annotations (no purity-check evidence exists; round1 cross-topic gap), and neither side has prototype evidence that laws on concrete functions survive lowering and are consumed end-to-end; (c) trait machinery adds verifier obligations (coherence, bound solving, D003 dyn-compatibility) that discharge no optimizer-consumed fact. Critically, the prosecution's own consistencyRisks concede provenance-grouped, compiler-checked cross-copy obligations — which is the contract-group half of interfaces reconstructed under another name. Both rulings therefore need the contract-grouping kernel; only the generic-body increment is genuinely contested.

## AI-writer considerations

Every writer-behavior claim on both sides is EMPIRICAL-UNTESTED — the corpus contains zero AI-codegen evidence. Admissible costs cut both ways. Cutting: O(N·K) source tokens per algorithm family, N-site change fan-out per edit inside the iteration loop, context inflation and stale-copy retrieval risk from near-duplicates, and the canonical drift substrate where a silently divergent regenerated copy plus a stale law annotation becomes an F003/N002-class miscompile channel. Keeping: a trait-resolution/coherence stage in every compile (unquantified; D001/D004 negative scopes), a hallucination surface in bounds/coherence/dyn-compatibility rules (D003) that discharge no optimizer fact, and non-local solver diagnostics (uncarded — no diagnostics evidence exists in the corpus at all). Neither magnitude is measured; the prosecution's deterministic-expander mitigation is undesigned, and the defense's claim that definition-site bound diagnostics shorten AI repair loops is asserted structurally, untested behaviorally. Citation-grade corrections found on review: C004 is a provisional direct extraction (feature-evidence-coverage.csv concurrency row: direct_source_extracted_needs_adversarial_verification), not workflow-confirmed 3-0 as the defense stated; and F004 attaches noalias-return per function — tying it to a Clone/Allocator-style interface contract is uncarded inference. The prosecution's reversibility claim is also only half-true: sugar can be added later, but writer-side concretization erases family identity from the artifact and forecloses the D005/F009 levers, so both directions carry irreversibility.

## What must be true

- Law/effect annotations must be compiler-checked (proof obligations or verified properties), not merely trusted: if trusted, both the N-copy alternative and per-impl trait laws are drift channels into F003/N002-class UB, and both sides' verification cases reduce to site-count arithmetic on unchecked assertions (misleading-comparisons note 5).
- Under any cut, the language must still provide the interface-shaped kernel both cases converge on: named contract groups (signature + law bundles) with compiler-checked per-member conformance and cross-copy annotation consistency; type-level capability predicates (C004-class, required by the round1 concurrency leading candidate); and an explicit closed-world dispatch construct (sum types + exhaustive match) — otherwise D009-style hand-rolled erasure and unchecked cross-copy drift follow. The prosecution's own risk list concedes this machinery, and it is currently undesigned.
- Under any keep, the kept core must be restricted to decidable, coherent, non-overlapping resolution with guaranteed static specialization as the only implicit path and dynamic dispatch only via the explicit D003-style restricted construct — matching, not reopening, the round1-dispatch leading candidate; open-world implicit dyn is out under every position heard.
- This feature ruling must not silently decide the monomorphization-vs-shared-body axis in either direction: round1-dispatch explicitly held it open pending measurement (zero quantitative cards on runtime, code size, i-cache, compile time), and writer-side full concretization is that axis decided in extremis.
- AI drift rates across regenerated near-duplicate copies versus error rates writing bounds/impls must be measured before writer-side concretization is relied on at scale — the load-bearing behavioral claims of both cases are currently unmeasured (zero AI-codegen evidence in corpus).
- The load-bearing dispatch cards (D001, D002, D003, D005, D007) must survive adversarial verification per the caveats.md standing rule before any final ruling hardens; feature-evidence-coverage.csv rates both dispatch rows low_to_medium confidence.
