# Round 3 — FFI fact attenuation (`ffi-attenuation`)

**Outcome:** `research_needed`

**Leading candidate:** Composite conservative-by-default declared wall (P3's barrier as repaired by critique + P1's residency + P2's vocabulary at its conservative bottom): every extern crossing carries a mandatory machine-readable declaration frame (reads/writes/escapes/threads/no-unwind/re-entry) — satisfying synthesis line 23 and round2-modules line 25 — but the only verifier-admissible frame is currently the maximally conservative one, preserving the line-33 no-third-state spine; C003-style barrier semantics at every crossing; any memory foreign code may retain must be PRE-DECLARED foreign-shared at its allocation site under the existing storage-contract floor (P1's arena residency and P3's demotion, repaired, are the same mechanism — compiler-inferred call-site demotion is rejected as a floor violation); mandatory LangHeap pointer-integer laundering ban / provenance rule as a forced companion decision; foreign unwinds abort at the boundary. Two knobs remain genuinely open and go to research, not debate: (a) whether non-foreign-shared LangHeap pointers may transiently cross under a no-retention obligation, and (b) whether per-function trusted relaxations are ever admitted — lawful only through the round-1 marked-assertion tier and only if sanitizer-class dynamic checkability is first carded. The honest envelope statement ships with it: D1 holds conditional on ABI-well-behaved foreign code and a delimited runtime TCB, identical across all designs.

## Rationale

The debate's real finding, surfaced by the critic and confirmed against the texts, is that the three positions are one design with different defaults: once P3's permanent demotion is repaired to respect the explicit-storage-contract and zero-cross-boundary-inference floors it becomes pre-declared foreign-shared allocation, which IS P1's arena residency, which IS P2's escape dimension at its conservative bottom; and the direct answer to the debate question is the same under all three — the wall prevents falsification of internal proofs by (1) the closed world eliminating every non-call channel (D008/D004/D007), (2) never letting the optimizer consume a fact whose truth quantifies over unseen foreign behavior (conservative frame + omission-is-sound, F003/N001), and (3) keeping foreign-retainable memory outside the proved fact domain by allocation-site declaration — while NOT preventing an ABI-misbehaved foreigner from scribbling on the address space, a residual every position shares and only uncarded hardware/OS isolation removes. D1 plus the round-2 floors force this skeleton decisively: the conservative default is round-1 trilateral consensus named in the threat model's own remedy column; the int-ptr ban is forced because without it barrier/quarantine imprecision becomes a soundness hole rather than an optimization loss; and trusted relaxations are currently inadmissible because they fail the round-1 marked-assertion tier's dynamic-checkability condition by P2's own evidence-gap list. But the outcome must be research_needed, not design_candidate_later: ffi_abi_runtime is missing_primary_source/priority-high and per decision-gates blocks promotion; every cost that would arbitrate the surviving knobs (copies vs barriers vs relaxations) is unquantified; the inbound direction, foreign-thread callbacks, and TCB delimitation are undesigned by all parties; and the corpus's own decided texts contradict each other at the wall (synthesis line 23 vs 33), requiring a formal amendment no verdict owns. The composite skeleton should be recorded as the leading candidate with pre-committed reopen triggers, which also discharges round2-modules' design-before-hardening ordering without violating the caveats standing rule.

## Position assessments (post-critique)

### Position 1 — Memory quarantine (copies + opaque handles; foreign arena; no LangHeap pointer crosses)

**Strengths:** Its core insight survives critique intact: because retention by foreign code is unverifiable (threat-model FFI row: verify 'no'), attenuation is monotone and permanent, so the only sound shapes are withdrawal or emptiness of the quantification domain. Its residency mechanism is the piece decided floors actually ratify — 'foreign-shared' as a writer-stated, allocation-site storage contract is one field on the existing round-2 memory floor, and it is exactly what P3's demotion becomes once repaired to respect the explicit-storage-contract and zero-cross-boundary-inference floors. It converts the boundary obligation into a syntactic check (no lang pointer in extern signatures) rather than a trusted semantic claim, which is the only shape compatible with the verification spine as written. Concessions were unusually honest (copy costs unquantified, ABI-axiom floor, erosion pressure, provenance coupling), and its framing of arena residency as 'relocating and pricing the tradeoff per-object by explicit writer choice' is the correct honest core.

**Weaknesses:** The headline theorem — attenuation identically zero, domain empty — is falsified by the position's own machinery, as the critic showed: copy buffers must be arena-resident (an unstated, unpriced double-copy discipline) or LangHeap becomes retainable; exported callback env-structs live in LangHeap under the round-2 closures floor; the handle table is a shared mutable language-side root. The control channel is unclosed: a foreign thread executing a language callback touches LangHeap by construction, and 'C001/C004 quantify over language threads only' is design stipulation, not card content (verified — neither card scopes to language threads; C004's confirmed scope is safe-Rust discipline with incorrect impls UB). Citation defect confirmed: F1 in verified-findings.md has no negative-scope field. The sole answer to its own charter-collision risk (arena residency for hot interop data) rests entirely on an uncarded GPU/heterogeneous precedent and forfeits F001/F002/F003 payoffs on exactly the data D0 exists for. The arena is not safety-free even if fact-free: two language threads racing through accessors is a D1-banned race, so checked-access vocabulary returns. Finally, D2 (2026-07-02) amends the 'verbosity is free' premise: marshalling code is now paid from a finite program token budget, unpriced.

### Position 2 — Declared trust contracts (per-extern effect/alias/lifetime/thread frames; conservative undeclared default; provenance-tainted trusted class)

**Strengths:** Its declaration VOCABULARY is the durable contribution: the threat-model FFI row names 'boundary contracts / conservative fallback' as the remedy verbatim, round2-modules line 25 makes contract-bearing signatures non-optional, and even P3 concedes any future scoped-escape relaxation must route through marked-assertion machinery — so every position eventually needs P2's schema (reads/writes/escapes/threads/no-unwind/re-entry). It is right that an ambient ABI-trust floor exists under every rival and that the ownership floor forces stating the post-call borrow state of anything passed out. C004's 2026-07-02 confirmation strengthens its best precedent: the reference bar itself ships with declared-surface residual unsoundness of exactly this shape. It is the only position with any audit/attribution story, and the only one that names process/SFI isolation as an expressible bottom tier — the sole mechanism that would strengthen rather than restate the D1 envelope.

**Weaknesses:** Its legality claim fails as stated: 'exactly one sanctioned third class' is unsupported — the verification spine verbatim denies any third state, so the position needs a formal spine amendment it pretends already exists, and it repurposes round2-modules line 25 while deleting that line's content (re-verified against BODIES every build — the redundancy IS the point; no body exists at the wall). It fails the admission condition of the round-1 tier it cites as license: marked assertions must be 'dynamically checkable in debug builds', and its own evidence-gap list concedes whole-execution wall properties (escape, thread-publication) have no carded runtime check and no sanitizer cards exist. Its forcing argument ('pure conservatism is inexpressible under the ownership floor') is refuted by the uniform post-state rule — treat every crossing as escape to pre-declared foreign-shared storage, which P1 and P3 both instantiate. The computable-taint auditability claim has no carded mechanism through lowering (misleading-comparisons #5; uncarded Rust-noalias incident history), and N001 implies the taint frontier can be the entire optimization horizon. Worst structural fact: the wall is the one place with no body to re-check a declaration against, so round2-modules' coherent-hallucination failure mode has, by definition, no redundancy channel here.

### Position 3 — Runtime barrier (conservative invalidation at each crossing; permanent demotion of escaped memory; sanitizer dev builds)

**Strengths:** Closest to what decided law already forces as the DEFAULT: the conservative-fallback branch of the threat-model remedy, trilaterally converged in round 1; C003 cards the barrier discipline as precedented production-compiler machinery, not novel apparatus; the omission-is-sound structure (F003 contrapositive, N001) gives crossings the correct failure asymmetry — imprecision costs optimization, not soundness (with the provenance caveat below). Its partition insight is the real cash value of the modules cut: the closed world makes the invalidation set bounded rather than total, preserving F001/F002-class facts at full strength over the FFI-unreachable interior. It preserves zero-copy with the dossier's named targets, and it composes cleanly with the abort-at-boundary unwind rule into one coherent wall semantics.

**Weaknesses:** 'Withdrawal is the unique lawful attenuation' is the mirror-image of P2's overclaim: the spine is a synthesis sketch item coupled to research_needed ir-strategy, not an enumerated floor, and line 23 of the same document says 'declared fact-attenuation'; its own concessions then reintroduce marked-assertion relaxations, reducing the disagreement with P2 to defaults and gating. Permanent demotion as specified violates the floors it invokes — a compiler-inferred, call-site-triggered rewrite of a writer-stated storage contract collides with the explicit-storage-contract floor, zero-cross-boundary-inference, and contract-edit gating, and is ownership-incoherent for borrowed crossings; repaired, it converges to pre-declared foreign-shared allocations, i.e., P1's residency. Its soundness story silently depends on the int-ptr/provenance ban it never states: without it, reachable-set completeness fails and 'imprecision never costs soundness' is false (threat-model row 2: 'often no'). C005 is overread (undef uses can be UB downstream; D1 forces atomic-grade demoted access regardless, which the risks section admits and the core argument denies). The cost headline is contradicted by its own concession that true declared contracts soundly recover optimization on fine-grained hot-loop FFI, and its staging mitigation IS copying. Sanitizer channel entirely uncarded and structurally partial. D2 also prices its 'verbose mechanical rewrites for free' claim.

## Key tradeoffs

- Facts vs copies vs trust — the actual triangle: residency/quarantine preserves F001/F002/F003 non-interference facts across every extern call for all LangHeap but forfeits all interior facts on foreign-resident data and pays unquantified copies; barrier/demotion preserves zero-copy but forfeits facts on the escaped set and pins motion at crossings; declared relaxations recover both at the price of an unverifiable trust surface with N001-unbounded blast radius when wrong. Zero cost quantification exists anywhere in the corpus to price any corner of this triangle (caveats.md).
- Failure asymmetry is the decisive soundness variable: partition/barrier/quarantine imprecision degrades optimization only IF the foreign-reachable set is a guaranteed over-approximation — which holds only under a LangHeap int-ptr laundering ban or provenance rule (threat-model row 2). Declared-contract imprecision is a silent, non-local miscompile. This asymmetry is what D1 forces the DEFAULT to be withdrawal-shaped; it does not by itself forbid a checkable marked-assertion tier later.
- Auditability vs nothing-to-audit: withdrawal designs have no trusted artifacts but also no attribution channel when the ambient ABI axiom is violated (foreign scribble manifests as a mis-attributed trap in innocent code); declaration designs have an enumerable audit surface but no carded taint mechanism through the pass pipeline. Post-violation attribution at the wall is undesigned under every position.
- The D1 envelope is identical across all three positions — 'impossible in accepted programs, conditional on ABI-well-behaved foreign code and the (undelimited) runtime TCB', Rust's shipped envelope — so advertised D1-composition or D1-by-construction differentiators are category errors (critic confirmed). Only hardware/OS isolation (process, MPK, CHERI) strengthens the claim itself, and it has zero cards; only P2 even names it as an expressible tier.
- Token budgets (new, unpriced by all parties): D2 (2026-07-02) amends round-2's 'verbosity is free' — quarantine's marshalling/accessor code and the barrier's staging rewrites are paid from the finite PROGRAM budget, while P2's declaration vocabulary is paid from the SPEC budget. The wall design now has a teachability cost dimension no advocate priced.
- Procedural deadlock: round2-modules line 24 requires the attenuation design BEFORE the closed-world commitment hardens, while caveats.md's standing rule plus ffi_abi_runtime (missing_primary_source, priority-high) forbid promoting any design now. The only lawful resolution is a provisional skeleton with pre-committed reopen triggers, not promotion — and the synthesis line-23 ('declared fact-attenuation') vs line-33 ('no third state') contradiction must be resolved by formal amendment, since every position was built by reading only one of the two lines.
