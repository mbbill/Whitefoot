- Three optimizer-fact channels are built and always on when facts are enabled: region-scoped intra-function alias metadata derived from borrow provenance; effect rows lowered to guaranteed function attributes; and checked algebraic laws licensing reassociation of user-operation reductions.
- Facts attach to declarations, not just definitions; their optimizations survive opaque boundaries without body visibility.
- A stated law the bound operation does not satisfy is refuted at compile time; a law is never trusted from the writer.
- The facts bundle is a single toggle; channel attribution is measured by differencing facts-on against facts-off on identical source.

## Facts

- 2026-07-09 rationale: the "no gain over Rust" audit was diagnosed valid-but-incomplete — it had measured only the channels rustc also emits (parameter noalias from borrow modes, arithmetic definedness), where parity was the corpus's own prediction; the delta channels are the ones with no Rust source channel at all, and the three of them were then built and measured within two days. (sourced)
- 2026-07-09 measurement: scoped-alias channel — the 8-column SoA kernel vectorizes guard-free from checker provenance (121 asm lines) where Rust's obvious shape needs loop-versioning (29 runtime guards, 2132 lines): ties at long trips, wins 2.0x at trip 8, and the 17x code-size delta is durable; even the expert inner-fn Rust idiom loses 1.17x at short trips. (sourced)
- 2026-07-09 measurement: checked-law channel — 3.3x over the obvious sequential fold on a saturating-add reduction, tying expert four-accumulator Rust; the same expert shape over signed saturating-add compiles silently to garbage in Rust while Whitefoot refutes the stated law at compile time — the cheat-proofness jewel of the three channels. See [[effects]] for the effect-attribute channel's own numbers. (sourced)
- 2026-07-10 statement: a fourth channel is carded, not built — blessed interpreter dispatch, lowering the naive loop-plus-match source shape to threaded/musttail dispatch, a structural delta Rust cannot express (no guaranteed tail calls, no computed goto); carding must cover branch-target-buffer behavior and CFG re-merging hazards before any spec or implementation work. (sourced)
- 2026-07-10 statement: facts on/off is neutral on single-buffer byte kernels (wc, base64 encode) — the channels need aliasing or effect pressure to matter, so single-array workloads are the wrong place to look for channel attribution. (sourced)
- 2026-07-12 statement: the throwaway frequency pilot over 42 crates found no current scoped-alias or checked-law opportunity surviving manual review, but optimized IR showed hot surviving bounds checks (101 first-party panic edges across 40 functions in one parser crate) — the next bounded bet is relational/precondition bounds proofs in a real workload, not more channel-frequency tooling. (sourced)
- 2026-07-14 research: future length, occupancy, generation, dynamic-borrow, shared-lifecycle, or refinement metadata becomes a fact channel whenever it authorizes payload access, uniqueness, destruction, allocation release, or check removal. Before shipping, each such channel must record an exact proposition, owner/provenance/version, producer, consumers, invalidators, transfer and speculation rules, facts-off semantic identity, surfaced artifact evidence, and hostile negative canaries. No initialization, occupancy, identity, dynamic-borrow, shared-lifecycle, or refinement fact is selected by this research. (sourced)
- 2026-07-15 research constraint: a proof-bearing ordinary module remains unprivileged only when every producer is admitted by the same fixed verifier and its certificate cannot add axioms, proof rules, effect or fact kinds, backend behavior, or foreign assumptions. Any accepted safety-relevant liveness or topology fact must remain bound to exact root, version, certificate, and dependency identities; ordinary abstract container invariants mint no fact unless explicitly checked and declared. The proposed admission gate and conditional proof-bearing fallback select no production fact channel. (sourced)
